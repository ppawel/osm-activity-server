About me

Get to know me better:

  1. I'm all about OpenStreetMap activity!

    Creating activities is easy - see server API.

    You can send me new activities and I will distribute them according to your wishes - to friends, nearby users, all users.

    You can attach any geometry to an activity - I will use it to respond to spatial queries!

  2. I'm here to serve!

    You can ask me (nicely!) to give you activities in different formats (JSON/RSS/HTML).

    Also, I accept many parameters such as bounding box, user id, size limit.

    See the Links section on the right for even more details.
